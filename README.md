# Mall Customer Segmentation -- Data Analysis Project

## Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze mall customer data to understand
customer demographics, income patterns, and spending behavior. The
analysis aims to identify meaningful insights about customer groups and
purchasing patterns which can help businesses design targeted marketing
strategies and improve customer engagement.

## Dataset

Dataset Features: • CustomerID -- Unique identifier for each customer •
Gender -- Gender of the customer • Age -- Age of the customer • Annual
Income (k\$) -- Customer annual income • Spending Score (1-100) -- Score
assigned by the mall based on spending behavior

## Project Steps

1.  Data Understanding
2.  Data Cleaning
3.  Outlier Detection
4.  Exploratory Data Analysis
5.  Filtering Analysis
6.  Sorting Analysis
7.  Customer Insights

## Exploratory Data Analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): The following visualizations were used:
• Gender distribution • Age distribution • Income distribution •
Spending score distribution • Income vs Spending Score relationship

## Filtering and Sorting

Filtering and Sorting Analysis: The dataset was filtered and sorted to
better understand customer behavior.

Filtering performed: • Male vs Female customer analysis • High spending
customers (Spending Score \> 60) • High income customers • Premium
customers (High income + High spending)

Sorting performed: • Top spending customers sorted by Spending Score •
Highest income customers sorted by Annual Income

## Key Insights

Key Insights: • The mall attracts both male and female customers. • Most
customers fall in the young to middle‑aged group. • Income levels vary
widely among customers. • Spending behavior differs significantly across
customers. • Premium customers (high income & high spending) represent
the most valuable segment. • Some of the highest‑income customers spend
very little, showing that income does not always translate into
spending.

## Conclusion

Conclusion: The analysis revealed different customer behaviors and
spending patterns. These insights help businesses better understand
their customers and design targeted marketing strategies to increase
engagement and revenue.
